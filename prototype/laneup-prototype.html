These are suggestions, not requirements. Each one explains the reason behind it so you understand the why, not just the what. Change them at your own pace.
Camera & Display
Camera lock
Unlocked
Lets you see more of the map. Harder at first but you'll react to threats much faster once you're used to it.
Minimap scale
Large
The minimap tells you where everyone is. Making it bigger means you're less likely to miss critical movement.
Floating damage text
On
Seeing damage numbers helps you understand how your champion scales as you buy items.
Controls
Attack move
A + Click
Tells your champion to attack the nearest enemy automatically —essential for ADC players especially.
Quick cast
Later, not now
Faster once you know your abilities well. Start on normal cast and switch when you feel confident.
Sound & Interface
Ally chat
Muted (optional)
As a new player, chat is often more distracting than helpful. Mute it and focus on the game first.
Announce pings
On
Your teammates communicate almost entirely through pings. Keep these on so you never miss a callout.
Allied HP bars
Always visible
Lets you see teammate health at a glance —important for knowing when to peel or disengage.
